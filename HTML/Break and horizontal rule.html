Short Poems

Weeping

by Federico GarcAa Larca


Weeping,
I ho down the street
Grotesque,without solution
With the sadness of Cyrano
And Quixote.

String Bass

by Adrian Green


Some like to dominate,
other careers
a voluptuous rhythm
on pliant string,
This pulse drives life
through wanton counterpoint,
the heart and harmony
of things.
